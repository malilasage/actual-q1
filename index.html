About

This website utilizes We Feel Fine, a computer program which traverses the internet for blogposts in which people write about their feelings. Feelings found are gathered in a database, along with the sentences they were found in, the location of the author at the time of publication, images, and more. My project takes the location entered and shows the 5 most blogged about feelings in the location entered. etc etc As said by the creators of We Feel Fine:At its core, We Feel Fine is an artwork authored by everyone. It will grow and change as we grow and change, reflecting what's on our blogs, what's in our hearts, what's in our minds.
